poem  ·  yiddish

My kids. My kids.

He walks. He waits. Counts the gates.
He seeks. He stays. Circles the maze.
No word. No sound. Just shadows and ground. He stops. He turns. No returns.
He scans. He slows. He names the rows.
My kids. My light. Out of sight. He walks. He waits. He waits.
