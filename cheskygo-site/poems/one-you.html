One you

A million species in one kind Thousand brains in one mind Countless wishes in a single heart A hundred directions light-years apart Eyeballs two, with eyes everywhere Almost blind, a vision unclear Cruising life down a one way Yet changing direction each day With fingers, ten, grasping my soul But again and again, zero control Billions of emotions, a vivid illusion Poor in reality, missing conclusion Numbers don't lie but are not always true Just try, and be the number one 'you'!
