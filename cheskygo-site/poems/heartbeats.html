Heartbeats

Songs of Life A Single Note Whispers of Silence, Rhythms of Hope.
Heaven’s music, A Unique Key— The Tone of a Beat, A Heart to Be.
A Hidden Song, A Silent Sound— Yearnings Along, Treasure Found.
Moments of Silence, Knocks of Hope, Music to Enjoy, On-beat to Cope.
A Hidden Gift, Yet Wrapped in Prayer— A Secret of Life For Now to Keep.
A Diamond to Shine, To Shimmer with Life, Yet to Be Carved, Prepared to Thrive.
Hashem and Us Two, Partners as One, Paving the Way ’Til Mashiach Comes.
One Day, We Will Cherish the New Song And Sing It Loud: “We Waited So Long.”
