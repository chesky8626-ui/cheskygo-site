Weaving Waves

Day and night Orange and lavender Weaved.
Sea and shore Water and wind Waved.
Whispers and knocks Foam and rocks Weaved.
Splash and retreat Embrace and release Waved.
Fire and frost Burn and chill Weaved.
Edge and endlessness Glow and gloom Waved.
Open and hide Torn and whole Weaved.
Drift and grip Hugs and goodbyes Waved.
Silence and scream Now and forever Weaved.
Cries and songs weave a wave. No wave is lost, no tide a waste. A tapestry woven of trials and braves. We live, we fall, we rise - we wave.
